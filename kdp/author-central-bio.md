# Author Central Setup — Soothemade

One-time setup at **authorcentral.amazon.com**. Apply to all 12 Soothemade books.

## Step-by-step

1. Sign in at authorcentral.amazon.com with the same Amazon account you used
   for KDP.
2. Claim "Soothemade" as the author. Author Central will ask you to verify
   ownership — it auto-detects the books published under your KDP account.
3. Paste the **Biography** below.
4. Upload an author image (use `brand/brand.svg` exported to PNG at 500×500, or
   a brand-styled photo if you have one).
5. Set up the **Author Page URL slug** — request `soothemade` if available.
6. Link any social / website URLs:
   - Website: `https://soothemade.com`
   - Newsletter: `https://notes.soothemade.com`
7. For each book that goes live: Author Central → Books → Add this book.

## Biography (paste into the "Biography" field)

```
Soothemade is a small apothecary of printables, planners, cards, and books for new parents. Founded in 2026 on a single belief: the gentlest companion through a hard season is a slightly older friend who has been there and will tell you the truth.

The Soothemade bibliography is written by Maya, a postpartum doula and mother of two. The books are short, plain, and built for the kind of week where you cannot keep track of anything.

Soothemade does not sell transformation. It does not sell unlocking your potential. It sells slow, considered companions for the parts of life no one is photographing.

soothemade.com
```

(~620 chars — comfortably under Author Central's 5000 limit)

## Author photo guidance

If you do not yet have a brand-styled photo:
- Use the Soothemade brand mark from `brand/brand.svg` (exported as PNG ≥500×500)
- Or: a photograph of a sage / cream still life (the brand language) without a
  human face — leaves the "Maya" identity unspecified, consistent with how the
  brand has been positioned

Avoid stock photos of women holding babies. Off-brand and contrived.

## After books are live

Author Central also lets you:

1. **Add editorial reviews** — paste in any 4-5 star reviews from ARC readers,
   doula colleagues, or press mentions
2. **Track sales rank history** — useful around free-promo runs to see the
   bestseller-rank bump
3. **Reply to customer reviews** — KDP discourages this but you can flag
   abusive/spam reviews here
4. **Schedule "Follow" notifications** — readers who follow your Author page
   get an email when you publish a new book
