## Launch sequencing rationale

- **Wave 1** is intentionally diverse across categories (cards, planner, guide, challenge) so we learn which *format* converts best, not just which topic.
- **Wave 2** loads up the high-WTP specialty segments (twins, NICU, c-section, working mom) once we've seen Wave 1 conversion data.
- **Wave 3** fills out the catalog with smaller-niche items and the bundle-fillers.
