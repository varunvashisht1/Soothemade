# 05 — Customer Journey Map

## Goal

Map the new-parent journey from pregnancy week 1 to child age 3. For each phase: emotional state, dominant searches, things they buy, things they wish existed. This becomes our publishing calendar AND product roadmap.

## Phases to cover

1. **Trying to conceive** (months pre-pregnancy)
2. **First trimester** (weeks 1–13)
3. **Second trimester** (weeks 14–27)
4. **Third trimester** (weeks 28–40)
5. **Birth + hospital stay** (~3 days)
6. **The fourth trimester** (weeks 0–12 postpartum)
7. **Newborn** (months 0–3)
8. **Infant** (months 4–12)
9. **One year old** (months 12–18)
10. **Older toddler** (months 18–36)

## Per-phase extractions

- Top 5 emotional states (worry, overwhelm, joy, exhaustion, isolation, etc.)
- Top 10 Google queries
- Top 5 things they purchase digitally
- Top 5 things they wish existed (gap)
- Best buyer segment within the phase (income, sophistication)

## Output

- `journey.md` — full 10-phase map with all per-phase tables
- `phase-priority.md` — which phases are most underserved + highest spend = best product targets
- `seasonal-overlay.md` — how the journey interacts with seasons (e.g., NYE-conception bumps, summer-newborn surge)
