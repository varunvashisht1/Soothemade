# 04 — Pinterest Trends

## Goal

Pinterest is the highest-leverage organic traffic channel for digital parent products. We need to know which queries are trending and what visual styles are winning.

## What I'm extracting

- Top 100 baby/parent search terms on Pinterest (via Pinterest Trends + search autocomplete)
- Rising queries (12-month upward trajectory)
- Seasonal spike calendar (e.g., "back to school printables" August, "New Year goal trackers" December)
- Visual style audit: dominant aesthetic (boho? minimal? bright? watercolor?), text-on-pin patterns
- Top-performing pin formats (video, carousel, static, infographic)

## Output

- `top-terms.md` — 100 search terms by visible engagement
- `rising-terms.md` — terms gaining momentum (highest priority for our pins)
- `seasonal-calendar.md` — month-by-month topical spikes
- `visual-style-audit.md` — winning aesthetics with notes for our Canva templates
- `pin-format-recipe.md` — what to make and when
