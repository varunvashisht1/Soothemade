I.
The studio

A small house. One room open. More being built.

Soothemade is a small studio of slow, considered things. We make planners, journals, printables, and cards for the seasons that move at the pace of weather, not the pace of work.

There is a kind of time that doesn't show up in any photograph. The hour before anyone else is up. The Tuesday with nothing planned. The recovery month no one warned you about. Most of a life is made of these hours.

We make one thing at a time. We finish it. We make the next one. Some weeks something ships. Some weeks it doesn't. It evens out the way a season does.
