Variant A · Deep Forest

The screenshot pick — forest green base, cream product cards.

Closest to what your browser rendered. Deep, slightly-warm forest green. Cream tincture-jar product cards stand out against it. Terracotta sits between them as the accent. Reads as a small herbal shop, after hours.

Variant B · Charcoal Luxe

The quieter cousin — near-black, dark cards, brighter botanicals.

Drop the green, drop the saturation, lean into charcoal with sage and warm terracotta accents. Product cards become dark inset frames with cream type. Reads more "modern indie luxury" than "small herbal shop" — Maude-coded, Aesop-coded.

Variant C · Mossy Oak

The warm middle — olive-brown undertone, cream cards, terracotta-forward.

Less "forest in winter," more "library in autumn." Warmer dark base with brown undertones, cream tincture-jar cards, the original terracotta brand color carrying more weight. Sage steps back; terracotta steps up. Closest to the existing brand identity, just inverted.
