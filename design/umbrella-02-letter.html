Soothemade a small studio
Spring · two thousand twenty-six

Dear stranger,

Thank you for finding us. This is a small studio. There is not very much here on purpose. We make slow, considered things — planners, journals, printables, card decks — for the seasons that move at the pace of weather, not the pace of work.

The tagline at the top of every page we write is made for the parts of life no one is photographing. Most of a life is made of those parts. The hour before anyone else is up. The Tuesday with nothing planned. The recovery month nobody warned you about. The walk you took because you couldn't sit still. The ones with photographs in them are the foam on top. We make things, mostly paper things, for the hours under the foam.

Soothemade is a small house with one room finished and a few more being built. The first room is open. The others are still being made, slowly, by the same hands.

We make one thing at a time. We finish it. We make the next one. Some weeks something ships. Some weeks it doesn't. It evens out the way a season does.

If you'd like to hear when a room opens — or when a letter arrives — there's a small form at the bottom of this page. Other than that, you owe us nothing.

— with care,

the studio
