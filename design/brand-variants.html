Brand mark — variant evaluation

Four variants of the Soothemade brand mark, sized to cater to different screen modes, print contexts, and theme backgrounds. Geometry is identical; only colors and the optional cream frame differ.

Soothemade · 2026-05-19

01

Primary — color on cream card

brand/brand.svg

The canonical mark

Sage sprig + italic Palatino wordmark on a baked-in cream #F5EFE6 card. The cream frame acts as a self-contained block — drop it on anything and the logo carries its own light surface with it.

Use for: product PDF covers, Etsy listing thumbnails, Pinterest pins where you want a contained block, business cards.

Avoid on: non-cream light backgrounds (creates a visible cream card edge), busy photos.
