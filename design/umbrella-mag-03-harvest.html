By the studio · Filed under welcome

Soothemade is a small studio of slow, considered things. We make planners, journals, printables, and cards for the seasons that move at the pace of weather, not the pace of work. The first room is open. More are being built, slowly, by the same hands.

There is a kind of time that doesn't show up in any photograph. The hour before anyone else is up. The walk you took because you couldn't sit still. The Tuesday with nothing planned. The recovery month no one warned you about.

Most of a life is made of these hours. The ones with the photographs in them are the foam on top. We make things — mostly paper things — for the hours under the foam.

We make one thing at a time. We finish it. We make the next one. The tools we use are tools, the way a sewing machine is a tool. They do not write your letter and they do not decide what you need.

Soothemade is small on purpose. If it gets larger, the voice you read here is what we are accountable to.

We make things at the pace of weather. Some weeks something gets finished. Some weeks it doesn't. It evens out the way a season does.
