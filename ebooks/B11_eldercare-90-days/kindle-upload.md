## 1 · Kindle eBook Details

**Language:** English (United States)

**Book Title:**
> Caregiving, the First 90 Days

**Subtitle:**
> For the Family Member Whose Life Just Changed. A Three-Month Companion for the Stroke, the Fall, the Diagnosis, the Discharge to "Now What."

**Series:** Soothemade · Book 11

**Author:** Soothemade

**Description:**

```
A book for the family member who has just had everything change.

The stroke. The fall. The dementia diagnosis. The hospital phone call. The discharge to "and now what." You did not sign up to be the primary contact, but the discharge nurse handed you the folder, and now you are.

This book is the three months that come next.

Inside:

• Week-by-week structure for thirteen weeks: medical team, paperwork, finances, housing decisions, family conversations
• The first-call list and the "who needs to know what" framework
• Medication-by-purpose log (no medication names — yours go in by hand, with your provider's input)
• Where they will live: a six-option decision framework
• The sibling-conflict workbook — old family wounds surface in caregiving, and this is the page for that
• The "I am not okay either" caregiver page
• Crisis-line page including Eldercare Locator (1-800-677-1116), Family Caregiver Alliance (1-800-445-8106), Alzheimer's Association (1-800-272-3900), Postpartum Support International caregiver track
• A chapter that most resources skirt: the grief, the resentment, the burnout

Written by Maya. Plain language. No "treasure these moments." No "it gets easier." Just the framework for the months that do not get easier yet.

If you are in crisis, please call Eldercare Locator or the Family Caregiver Alliance. The book is not a substitute for support.

Part of the Soothemade bibliography. Pair with The Long Caregiving Stretch (Book 12) for the months and years after.
```
