**Series:** Soothemade · Book 9

**Description:**

```
Your partner is pregnant. You are also pregnant, in the way that matters for the relationship. You did not get the body changes. You did get the rest of it: the appointment schedule, the fear, the financial recalculation, the grief no one names, the friendship-rebuild that is coming, and the slow shift in who you are about to be.

This is the prequel to The Partner's Postpartum Playbook. The nine months before the baby. Written to the non-birthing partner — same voice, same plain language, same refusal to tell you to "be more supportive" without telling you how.

Inside:

• First trimester: what's happening for her, what's happening for you, what to say at "I'm tired" and "I'm scared"
• Second trimester: the planning chapter, the relationship chapter, the work + money conversation
• Third trimester: hospital bag for you, the version of the birth plan that is for you, what your job is in the room
• The partner's own grief and fear chapter — the page most partners do not give themselves time for
• 30 "things to say" scripts and 15 "things to never say" reminders
• A chapter on the relationship you are both inside, and how to keep it standing

Written by Maya, a postpartum doula. For the partner who wants to show up well during the nine months, not just the four trimesters after.

Part of the Soothemade bibliography. Read alongside The Partner's Postpartum Playbook.
```
