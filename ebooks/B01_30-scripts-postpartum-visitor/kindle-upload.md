## 6 · Launch sequence (recommended)

After approval (24-72 hours):

1. **Day 1-5: Free promo run.** KDP Select gives you 5 free days per 90-day term. Burn them upfront. Goal: 500-2000 downloads, ~20 reviews. Promote via r/BabyBumps, r/Mommit, postpartum doula Facebook groups, your personal network.
2. **Day 6+: Raise to $4.99.** Buyers from the free run who liked it leave reviews. Reviews → Kindle search rank → organic discovery.
3. **Day 30: Check the data.** If KU page-reads + paid sales > $50, this product has legs. If under $10, the title/cover/sample chapter needs work, not the book.
4. **Day 90: Decide on KDP Select renewal.** If KU is paying, renew. If not, drop exclusivity and list on Kobo / Apple Books / Google Play in parallel.
