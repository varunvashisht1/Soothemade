# OUTLINE — B01

## Title

**30 Scripts for the Postpartum Visitor**
*What to say when you don't have the energy to find your own words*

By Maya | Soothemade Press

## Structure

### Front matter

- **Title page**: Title, subtitle, author, imprint
- **Copyright page**: © 2026 Soothemade Press / Soothemade. Personal use. Crisis-line reference.
- **Dedication**: "For the parent at the door, in the third day, in the fourth trimester."
- **Table of Contents**: Generated from headings
- **A note from Maya**: ~300 words, expanded from the PDF intro

### Chapters

1. **Why "No" Is Hard Postpartum** (~600 words). The chapter the PDF didn't have room for. Frames the whole book: postpartum exhaustion eats words first, the visitor-management problem is universal, "no" is a complete sentence.

2. **Hospital Visitors** (intro ~200 words + 5 scripts verbatim). The doorway. The nurses. The "I had no idea" cousin.

3. **The First Week at Home** (intro ~200 words + 5 scripts verbatim). The doorbell. The "just dropping off a meal" person. The cousin with a cough.

4. **Unsolicited Advice** (intro ~250 words + 8 scripts verbatim). Why the comments hurt more postpartum. The "in my day" person.

5. **Visitor-Pressure Family** (intro ~250 words + 6 scripts verbatim). The hardest chapter — the people who love you and refuse to hear no.

6. **Photos and Social Media** (intro ~150 words + 3 scripts verbatim). The grandparent who already posted. The relative who wants a "professional shoot ASAP."

7. **Body and Emotional Commentary** (intro ~150 words + 3 scripts verbatim). "You look tired." "Are you enjoying every minute?"

8. **Templates for the Bigger Conversations** (intro ~200 words + 5 templates verbatim). The pre-baby family text. The "I changed my mind" follow-up. The hard conversation.

9. **How to Use These Scripts** (~400 words). Print them. Modify them. Share with your partner. Don't justify. Repeat. Forgive yourself for changing your mind.

10. **A Last Note** (~300 words). The people who get hurt. The ones who reveal themselves. The "either outcome is useful" line.

### Back matter

- **About Maya**: ~100 words, fictional-founder framing per brand persona.
- **About Soothemade Press**: ~100 words, the imprint's mission.
- **Also from Soothemade**: cross-sell to the printable version (P02) + adjacent products (P04, P15, P49, P55).
- **Crisis-line page**: PSI, MMH hotline, 988, international equivalents.
