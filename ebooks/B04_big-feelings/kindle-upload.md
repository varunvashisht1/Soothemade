**Description:**

```
A field guide for the toddler who is on the kitchen floor and cannot say why.

You already know the moment. The juice cup was the wrong color. The sock has a wrinkle. You said "yes" to the wrong "yes." And now you are kneeling on linoleum next to a small person whose face is the color of strawberries, and the calm-down language you read about online is doing exactly nothing.

This book is the language for that floor.

Inside:

• 40 short cards across four packs
• 10 "name the feeling" cards in toddler-shaped words
• 10 "where it lives in the body" cards (tight chest, hot face, hands that need to push)
• 10 calm-down tool cards (the kind that actually work at 2 and 3, not the deep-breathing ones for older kids)
• 10 reset and repair cards for after
• A parent note at the back, for what to say to yourself when you've just yelled

Written by Maya, a postpartum doula and mother of two who has been on the kitchen floor too. Plain language. No "your child is dysregulated." No shame.

Part of the Soothemade bibliography. Slow, considered companions for the parts of life no one is photographing.
```
