# P40 — Rainbow Baby (Pregnancy After Loss) Planner

A 30-page planner for someone pregnant after a previous loss. Honors the previous loss without making the new pregnancy about it. Covers the extra anxiety, the extra appointments, the anniversary handling, the "telling people" hardness, and the "what if it happens again" page that exists so the planner is not avoiding the question.

## Why this exists

Pregnancy after loss is its own genre of experience. The body is doing something common. The mind is doing something different. Every cramp is a memory. Every appointment is the previous appointment. Most pregnancy planners assume no prior loss and read as careless to a parent who lost a previous baby.

This planner assumes the prior loss. It is not a grief book (P20 Pregnancy Loss Gentle Companion is the grief book). It is the planner that holds both the previous loss and the current pregnancy at the same time.

## YMYL care

- Cover YMYL banner.
- The previous loss is acknowledged at the top, then honored throughout.
- Memorial pages are optional and clearly marked.
- The "anxiety in pregnancy after loss" page does NOT prescribe management strategies. It names common patterns and routes to perinatal mental-health care.
- The "if it happens again" page exists because pretending the question is not there is worse than naming it.
- All medical signs route to the provider.
- Crisis lines listed.

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- "The previous loss" acknowledgment page
- Memorial pages (3, optional)
- Anxiety in pregnancy after loss page (perinatal mental-health route)
- Extra appointment tracking pages
- Trimester one emotional checkpoint (the trimester of dread)
- Trimester two emotional checkpoint (the trimester of "starting to hope")
- Trimester three emotional checkpoint (the trimester of "almost")
- Anniversary handling page
- Telling-people page
- "When fear shows up" grounding page
- Partner page
- The "what if it happens again" page (sober, present, kind)
- Crisis line page
- A final letter
