# P53 — Trimester-by-Trimester Emotional Companion

A 30-page slow companion to pregnancy, organized by trimester rather than by week. Focuses on the emotional side: identity shifts, pre-baby grief, the social-life changes, the body-as-experience (not data). Companion to P08 (the data-heavy weekly journal) by intentional design contrast.

## Why this exists

P08 is the data-heavy week-by-week journal — symptoms, milestones, the body's metrics. P53 is the opposite: a slow, prompted, feelings-focused journal across the three trimesters. Many pregnant people want both, or just this one. The brand has been weighted toward planners and tracking products; this is a quieter journal for the emotional work.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- Mental-health support routing exists for the "if you are not okay" page.
- Pre-baby grief is named openly (an underspoken truth in pregnancy).

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- "This is the slow journal" framing page
- Trimester One section (5 prompted pages)
- Trimester Two section (5 prompted pages)
- Trimester Three section (5 prompted pages)
- "If you are not okay" page
- A final letter
