Most pregnancy planners track symptoms, weight, appointments, kicks. This one tracks the feelings.

Identity shifts. The grief for the version of you before. The social-life changes. The strangers commenting on your body. The fear-and-joy mix. The body as experience, not data. Five slow pages per trimester. Prompts that no chart has room for.

This is the journal you write in slowly, on a Saturday morning, with tea. Not the one you fill in at the appointment.

## What's inside

Thirty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.**
- **"This is the slow journal" framing page.**
- **Trimester One section** (5 prompted pages): the secret-keeping, the identity shift, body changes from the emotional side, what you are grieving from before, the letter to pre-pregnant you.
- **Trimester Two section** (5 prompted pages): going public, the social shifts, body deepening, being visibly pregnant in the world, the strangers, the friend group changing.
- **Trimester Three section** (5 prompted pages): the waiting, nesting examined, fear-and-joy, "I miss being just me" permission, what to remember about being this pregnant.
- **"If you are not okay" page.**
- **A final letter.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Personal use only.
