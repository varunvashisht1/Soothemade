# P50 — Family Caregiver Workbook

A 40-page workbook for the long-haul caregiver providing ongoing care for an aging parent, a partner with chronic illness, or an adult family member who requires it. Sister-product to P46 (which covers the first 90 days). This one is for months 4 and beyond.

## Why this exists

P46 covers the acute first 90 days after a major change. P50 covers the long stretch after. The months and years when the caregiver is now in a sustained role with no clear end date. The challenges are different: respite, the slow erosion of the caregiver's own health, the marital strain, the career compromise, the sibling dynamics that turn into permanent patterns, the "I cannot do this forever" reckoning.

## YMYL care

- Family/YMYL careful.
- No medication names.
- Mental-health crisis lines listed (caregivers have well-documented elevated rates of depression, anxiety, substance use, and self-neglect).
- All clinical decisions route to the medical team.
- Financial and legal decisions route to professionals.

## Scope

Approximately 40 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- "You are doing real work" identity-validation page
- Updated medical team page
- Medication log by purpose (re-printable monthly as meds change)
- Monthly care-team coordinator log
- Insurance + billing tracker
- Respite-care planning page (essential for sustainability)
- The "I am angry" page
- The "I am exhausted" page
- The "I cannot do this forever" page
- Sibling-conflict workbook (4 pages)
- "What they would want, if they could tell me" page
- Marriage / partnership maintenance page
- Job and career considerations
- Your own health page
- Financial caregiving + hospice considerations
- Caregiver crisis lines
- A final letter
