# P56 — Letters to Baby Keepsake Journal

A 40-page pregnancy keepsake journal. Thirteen prompted letters to the unborn baby across pregnancy, plus a "things I want to remember to tell you" running list, a pocket page for ultrasound photos and the hospital bracelet, and space to keep.

## Why this exists

The catalog has a lot of practical pregnancy products. This is the heart-y one. The lyrical pregnancy keepsake. The journal a person fills in slowly across the nine months and gives to their child at eighteen, or saves on their own shelf, or rereads on the kid's wedding day.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- The single mental-health-routing reminder appears in the front matter for completeness.

## Scope

Approximately 40 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- 13 prompted letters with significant blank-page writing space:
  - Letter 1: Before I knew you
  - Letter 2: The day I found out
  - Letter 3: The first appointment
  - Letter 4: Telling people about you
  - Letter 5: The first time I felt you
  - Letter 6: Your name, the story
  - Letter 7: What our family is like before you arrive
  - Letter 8: What the world is like in this year
  - Letter 9: What your other parent is like
  - Letter 10: What I want you to know about your grandparents
  - Letter 11: The last week before you
  - Letter 12: The night before you came
  - Letter 13: The first time I saw you
- "Things I want to remember to tell you" running list (2 pages)
- Pocket pages for the ultrasound photo + hospital bracelet
- A final note
