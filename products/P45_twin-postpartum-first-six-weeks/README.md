# P45 — Twin Postpartum, the First Six Weeks

A 40-page postpartum planner specifically for twin parents in the first six weeks home. Companion to P33 (the pregnancy planner). Covers the tandem feeding, the double bottle/burp logistics, the rotation between two parents and two babies, and the village volume.

## Why this exists

P07 Newborn Survival Planner covers the first six weeks for one baby. The twin version is materially different. Tandem feeds. Two of every overnight diaper change. The "who is on which baby" rotation. The doubling of every visit, every laundry load, every "is this normal" question. Twin postpartum gets one-baby content with twin words pasted on, and that doesn't work.

This is the planner for the second-half of the twin journey.

## YMYL care

- Postpartum/YMYL careful (multifetal postpartum has higher rates of postpartum depression and anxiety than singleton postpartum, and that needs to be named).
- No medication names. Pain management, milk supply, recovery all routed to provider.
- Perinatal mental-health line listed at the front (PSI: 1-833-852-6262 US) given the elevated risk.
- NICU section included because a meaningful share of twin parents will have NICU time.

## Scope

Approximately 40 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages, "what's different for twin postpartum" page
- Day-by-day for first 7 days
- Week-by-week for weeks 2-6 (5 weekly pages)
- Daily feed + diaper log template (one-page, designed for two babies side-by-side)
- The "who has which baby" rotation page
- The pump-with-twins page
- Visitor management (twin parents need a different kind of visitor)
- Partner page (twin-postpartum specific)
- "If still NICU" page
- The "I am not okay" page
- Mental-health crisis lines
- A final letter
