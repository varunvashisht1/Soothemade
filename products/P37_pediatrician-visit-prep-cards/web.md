The well-baby visits come fast in the first two years. Twelve appointments, roughly, in the standard US schedule. Each one with its own list of measurements, milestones, conversations, and vaccines. Each one you walk into tired and walk out of without quite remembering what was said.

These cards are the prep + record. One card per visit. Print on cardstock. Bring the right one to the appointment.

## What's inside

Twelve cards, one per visit, plus the "what to expect at every visit" page and a "when to call between visits" page.

Each visit card has:

- **Approximate age** for the visit
- **What the pediatrician usually checks** at that visit
- **What to prepare** in advance (questions, things to remember, items to bring)
- **What to ask** (a starting list)
- **What to write down after** (the measurements, the recommendations, the next step)
- **Red flags to watch for** between this visit and the next one

The cards do not name vaccines. Vaccines are described as "the routine vaccines for this visit" and the pediatrician will tell you the current schedule.

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Print on cardstock if you can. Slip the next visit's card in the diaper bag. Personal use only.
