# P03 — The Slow Motherhood Weekly Planner

> Wave 1 launch product. Brand-defining. Pinterest "slow motherhood" +310% YoY.

## Product summary

36-page, 4-week undated planner. Designed against the productivity-planner template: 3 list slots/day not 10, 2 daily questions not 20, no morning routine prescriptions, no habit tracker, no toxic positivity.

- **Pages:** 36
- **Price:** $12 Etsy / $12 Gumroad
- **Buyer:** moms 0–5y (broad), the burnt-out-on-planners audience
- **Format:** PDF, US Letter + A4

## Why this is the brand-defining product

If a buyer never opens any other product from us, the Slow Motherhood Planner should communicate the entire brand voice and aesthetic. It IS the brand promise.

Voice rules from `brand/voice-guide.md` are at their most concentrated here. Every line should pass: *"Would Maya/we say this out loud to a friend?"*
