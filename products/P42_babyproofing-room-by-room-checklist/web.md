Babyproofing happens in a window. Too early, and you have outlet covers in a house with no baby that crawls. Too late, and your baby is reaching for the cleaning cabinet you forgot.

This checklist is the pre-crawling pass. Roughly 8 to 10 months for most. Go room by room. Mark off what is done. Decide what to fix this weekend, and what to fix next week.

## What's inside

Fourteen pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start** and **when to do the babyproofing pass**
- **Living room** hazards
- **Kitchen** hazards (the most dangerous room for most households)
- **Bathroom** hazards
- **Bedrooms** (parent + baby)
- **Stairs + hallways**
- **Outdoor + yard + garage**
- **Vehicles**
- **Poison + emergency prep** (with US Poison Control number)
- **The "I cannot do all this today" page** that ranks the most urgent fixes
- **A final note**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Personal use only.
