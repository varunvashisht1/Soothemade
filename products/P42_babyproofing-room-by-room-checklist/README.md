# P42 — Babyproofing Room-by-Room Checklist

A 14-page checklist for the babyproofing pass that needs to happen before the baby moves (around 8 to 10 months for most). Goes room-by-room.

## Why this exists

Most babyproofing content reads as advertising for specific products. This checklist names the hazards and describes the type of solution by category, without endorsing a brand. The buyer figures out what works for their house and budget.

## YMYL care

- Low-YMYL.
- No specific product recommendations.
- Poison Control number included (US: 1-800-222-1222).
- Routes medication-storage to the parent's own provider for what's safe and how to store it.

## Scope

Approximately 14 pages:

- Cover, note, how-to-use, timing page
- Living room
- Kitchen
- Bathroom
- Bedrooms (parent + baby)
- Stairs + hallways
- Outdoor + yard + garage
- Vehicles
- Poison + emergency prep
- The "I cannot do this all today" prioritization page
- A final note
