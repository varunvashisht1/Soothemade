## Notes on differentiation

Most "screen-free activity" PDFs on Etsy are giant lists (100 ideas!) — overwhelming, never used, race to the bottom. Our differentiation is the **deck format + challenge structure** + **anti-moralistic positioning**. The "not a detox" angle is the wedge.
