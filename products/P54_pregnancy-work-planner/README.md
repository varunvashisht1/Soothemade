# P54 — Pregnancy + Work Planner

A 30-page planner for navigating pregnancy at work. Pre-announcement preparation, the manager conversation, HR navigation, accommodations, mat-leave prep, return-to-work pre-plan, the "what if I don't want to come back" page.

## Why this exists

Most pregnancy guidance assumes a stay-at-home transition. Most postpartum guidance assumes you are not currently working. The actual experience of pregnancy AT work, telling the boss, asking for accommodations, planning the handoff, deciding about return, has no widely-used resource. This planner fills that.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- Provider letters for accommodations are referenced; specifics route to provider.
- US-specific labor laws (FMLA, PWFA, ADA, PUMP Act) referenced by name; international users routed to their country's equivalents.
- Pay + benefits considerations routed to HR / employment attorney.

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- Pre-announcement: company policy + legal rights research page
- When-to-tell decision page (timing, by trimester)
- Script for telling your manager
- Script for the HR conversation
- Accommodations checklist
- Mat-leave prep (handoff doc template, ramp-down plan, out-of-office)
- Mid-leave check-in page (life admin while you are off)
- Return-to-work pre-plan
- The "what if I don't come back" page
- A final note
