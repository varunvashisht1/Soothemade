# P36 — Sleep-Disruption Pattern Journal

A two-week pattern-finding journal for parents of babies and toddlers (roughly 3 months to 3 years) whose sleep has changed and they want to spot what is happening before they decide what to do about it.

## Why this exists

Sleep training is the YMYL bear-trap of the parenting niche. Soothemade does not prescribe a method. But many parents need a way to see their kid's actual pattern, not just remember it through exhaustion, before they make any decisions. This journal is the seeing tool. It does not tell the parent what to do.

## YMYL care

- The journal is **explicit** about not being a method. The "this journal does not tell you what to do" page is page four.
- No sleep-training methods are named. No "cry it out", no "no cry", no specific approach.
- No medication mentions.
- The "when to call the pediatrician" page is prominent.
- The "what we already tried" page lets the parent track their own attempts without endorsing any of them.

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages including:

- Cover, three front-matter pages, the "this is logging, not method" page
- Before-you-start baseline page
- 14 daily log pages (two weeks of day + night logs)
- Two weekly-review pattern-spotting pages
- "What might be happening this developmental stage" reference page (general, not prescriptive)
- "Common regression ages" reference page (just info)
- Partner page
- When to call the pediatrician
- "What we already tried" page
- A final note
