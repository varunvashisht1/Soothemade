Sleep has changed. You are tired in a new way. The internet has fifty methods for sale and you do not want to buy any of them today.

This journal is for the step before the decision. Two weeks of logging what is actually happening, side by side, so when you and your partner and your pediatrician talk about it, you have data, not a memory shaped by exhaustion.

It is not a method. It is the page where you can see the pattern.

## What's inside

Thirty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.** Why this is logging and not a method.
- **The "this journal does not tell you what to do" page.** Explicit framing.
- **Baseline page.** Where you are starting from.
- **Fourteen daily log pages.** Each page covers one day and one night, side by side.
- **Two weekly-review pages.** Pattern-spotting prompts.
- **"What might be happening" reference page.** Common developmental ages and what often shifts at each, without telling you what to do.
- **"Common regression ages" reference page.** Plain information.
- **Partner page.** For the support person.
- **When to call the pediatrician.** Specific triggers.
- **"What we already tried" page.** Your record, without judgment.
- **A final note.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Print, fill in. Re-print for the next stretch, if there is one. Personal use only.
