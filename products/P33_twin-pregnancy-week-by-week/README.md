# P33 — Twin Pregnancy Week-by-Week Planner

A 60-page, week-by-week planner for people pregnant with twins, from week 6 through delivery. Built because most pregnancy planners assume a singleton and don't talk about extra growth scans, the higher-monitoring rhythm, the NICU possibility, the help-schedule reality, or the emotional weight of carrying two.

## Why this exists

Most twin pregnancies are uncomplicated. A meaningful share are not. Either way, the appointment rhythm is different, the body experience is different by trimester three, and most planning resources don't translate. This pack is the thing the twin-pregnancy parent needs that the singleton planners don't cover.

## YMYL care

- Multifetal pregnancy is a medical situation. The pack does **not** name medications, **not** prescribe a delivery method, **not** make sleep or feeding decisions. Everything routes to the provider.
- Cover carries the standard YMYL banner.
- Body content includes the "this is a planning tool, not medical advice" disclaim near the front and at every appointment-decision page.
- Crisis lines for perinatal mental health are not at the top (this is not a mental-health product) but a single "if you are scared" route is included.

## Scope

Approximately 60 pages including:

- Cover, two front-matter pages, "what's different about twins" page
- Trimester 1 intro + weekly pages for weeks 6-13
- Trimester 2 intro + weekly pages for weeks 14-27
- Trimester 3 intro + weekly pages for weeks 28-40 (most twin parents deliver by 37)
- Appointment + growth-scan prep template (recurring)
- Hospital bag page (twin-specific differences)
- The "if NICU" page
- Postpartum first 24 hours with two
- Partner / support-person page
- Village help-schedule template
- A final letter
