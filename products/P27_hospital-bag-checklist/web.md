The hospital-bag lists on the internet are mostly affiliate marketing. They contain forty-three items, twenty-six of which you will not use, and seven of which the hospital actually provides.

This checklist is the one I packed from on my second baby, after the first had taught me what was actually needed and what was not.

## What's inside

Eight pages, printable or fillable:

- **The one-page summary**, for a friend, partner, or in-law who has to grab the bag if you are not there when the call comes.
- **For her**: the actual minimum, plus the comfort items that make hour seventeen better.
- **For the partner**: separate, because a partner with their own snack bag is a partner who can stay.
- **For the baby**: short. The hospital provides most of it.
- **For going home**: the car-seat reminder, the going-home outfit, the pre-warmed-car note.
- **The "two weeks before" page**: install the car seat. Practice it. Do the route in daylight.
- **The phone-line list**: who to call, in what order, and what each one's number is.
- **The "don't bother" page**: the things you packed last time and didn't use.

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Personal use only.
