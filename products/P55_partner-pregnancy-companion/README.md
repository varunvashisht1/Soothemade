# P55 — The Partner-During-Pregnancy Companion

A 30-page companion written FOR the non-birthing partner DURING pregnancy. Different from P04 (which is the partner's postpartum playbook). Covers what the pregnant person is experiencing each trimester, how to be useful, the partner's own grief and fear, the appointment-attendance role, scripts.

## Why this exists

P04 covers the partner's postpartum role. Pregnancy is its own nine-month role that has very few resources directed at the partner. This product fills that gap: a companion the partner reads to know what is happening in their loved one's body, what to do when nausea is bad, when to go to which appointment, how to handle their own grief and fear as a non-birthing parent, and how to maintain the relationship through the change.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- Routes specific medical questions to provider throughout.
- A "partner mental health is real" page acknowledges that partners can experience prenatal depression and anxiety too, with crisis lines.

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- "You are also doing something" framing
- Trimester One for the partner (4 pages)
- Trimester Two for the partner (4 pages)
- Trimester Three for the partner (4 pages)
- Your own grief + fear page
- Appointment-attendance role page
- Things to say + things NOT to say
- "Your relationship is changing" maintenance page
- Partner mental health page
- A final letter
