You are not the one growing the baby. You are the one growing alongside, in your own way, with a different set of jobs.

The pregnant-person planning industry mostly talks past you. The friends mostly ask about your partner. The appointments are about her body. Your role is real, and your experience is real, and almost no one has written a playbook for you.

This is the playbook. What's happening for your pregnant partner each trimester. How to be useful. Your own grief and fear that you may not have given language to yet. The appointments you should be at. The things to say. The things to not say. The relationship you are both inside, and how to keep it standing.

## What's inside

Thirty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.**
- **"You are also doing something" framing.**
- **Trimester One** for the partner (4 pages).
- **Trimester Two** for the partner (4 pages).
- **Trimester Three** for the partner (4 pages).
- **Your own grief + fear page.**
- **Appointment-attendance role.**
- **Things to say + things NOT to say.**
- **"Your relationship is changing" maintenance page.**
- **Partner mental health page.**
- **A final letter.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Personal use only.
