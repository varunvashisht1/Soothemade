You are about to take a small person on a long trip. The car ride. The flight. The four-hour airport layover. The "they will not sit still for one more minute."

This pack is the printable bag of tricks. Eight scavenger hunts (different for car and plane). I Spy templates. Conversation starters for the long quiet stretches. Story prompts the parent reads. Sing-along lyrics that you already half-remember. Drawing pages. A sticker chart. And the "if they're losing it" page for the bottom of hour three.

## What's inside

Thirty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start** and **what to pack in the activity bag.**
- **Pre-trip prep checklist.**
- **Day-of prep.**
- **Eight scavenger hunts.** Four for car trips, four for flights / airports.
- **Four I Spy templates.**
- **Conversation starters page.**
- **Five story-starter prompts.**
- **Sing-along lyrics** (five traditional songs).
- **Three drawing-prompt pages.**
- **Sticker chart** (parent provides stickers).
- **The "if they're losing it" page.**
- **Trip-memories page.**
- **A final note.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Print and put in the activity bag. Personal use only.
