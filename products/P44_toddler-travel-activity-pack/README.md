# P44 — Toddler Road Trip + Flight Activity Pack

A 30-page activity pack for road trips and flights with toddlers (roughly 2 to 5). Parent prep on one side, kid activities on the other.

## Why this exists

Travel with toddlers is a planning problem. The two-hour stretch in the car. The four-hour flight. The "everyone runs out of snacks at hour three." This pack has the parent's prep checklist and the kid's activity surface, both printable.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- Activities are language-light enough that pre-readers can do most with parent help.
- No medication mentions ("the bring-for-trips medical bag" page lists categories but no names, defers to provider).

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, parent note, how-to-use, the "what to pack" page
- Pre-trip prep checklist
- Day-of prep
- Eight scavenger hunt sheets (4 for car, 4 for flight/airport)
- Four I Spy templates
- Conversation starters page (for the long stretch)
- Five story-starter prompts
- Sing-along lyrics (5 traditional songs)
- Three drawing-prompt pages
- A sticker chart (parent provides stickers)
- The "if they're losing it" emergency-soothe page
- Trip-memories page (filled in after)
- A final note

## Companion products
