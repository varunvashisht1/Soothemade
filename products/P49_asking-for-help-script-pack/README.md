# P49 — "Asking for Help" Script Pack

A 20-page small product with 30 scripts for asking specific people for specific help across postpartum, parenting, eldercare, and ordinary adult life. Companion to P02 Saying No Scripts.

## Why this exists

P02 covered scripts for saying NO. The inverse is harder: actually asking for help. Most people who need help do not ask, because asking is socially loaded. This pack lowers the barrier by giving the exact words.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL.
- Scripts for asking for money + emotional support included; both note that professional help (financial planner / therapist) is also a path.

## Scope

Approximately 20 pages:

- Cover, parent note, how-to-use, "the structure of a good ask" page
- 30 scripts across 8 categories:
  - Meals (5)
  - Childcare (5)
  - Eldercare (3)
  - Errands (3)
  - Emotional support (4)
  - Money (3)
  - Home help (4)
  - Visit (3)
- "When they say no" page
- "When they say yes" page
- Thank-you script library
- A final note
