Asking for help is harder than saying no.

The "no" pack gives you the words to decline. This one gives you the words to ask. Specific people. Specific help. Specific timing. Because "let me know if you need anything" is the worst offer in the language, and "could you bring lasagna on Thursday at 6" is what actually moves help into your kitchen.

## What's inside

Twenty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.** Why asking is hard.
- **The structure of a good ask.** Specific person, specific request, specific time, easy way to say no.
- **Thirty scripts** across eight categories:
  - Meals (5 scripts)
  - Childcare (5)
  - Eldercare (3)
  - Errands (3)
  - Emotional support (4)
  - Money (3)
  - Home help (4)
  - Visit (3)
- **"When they say no" page.**
- **"When they say yes" page.**
- **Thank-you script library.**
- **A final note.**
