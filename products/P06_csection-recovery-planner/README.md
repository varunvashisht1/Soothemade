# P06 — C-Section Recovery Planner

> Wave 2 product. High-WTP segment (specific desperate buyers, low competition). YMYL-adjacent.

## Product summary

5-phase (weeks 1, 2, 3, 4, 5–6) recovery planner built specifically for c-section postpartum — not adapted from a vaginal-birth planner. Includes medication tracker, incision check log, partner handoff page, mental-health watch by week, and a 6-week appointment prep page.

- **Pages:** 36
- **Price:** $14 (premium pricing reflects high WTP)
- **Buyer:** scheduled and emergency c-section moms + their partners
- **Format:** PDF

## YMYL note

This product touches surgical recovery, which is YMYL territory. Voice carefully avoids prescriptive language ("what's generally happening" not "what to do"). Disclaimers are prominent. Owner should re-read `content.md` fully before publishing — and ideally have a c-section mom friend review.
