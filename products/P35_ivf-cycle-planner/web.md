IVF is a process the clinic understands and a process you live through. They have the data. You have the days.

This planner is for the days. The injection times. The bloodwork numbers your clinic sent you. The follicle count at each scan. The thing the nurse said on the phone you want to remember. The cost running quietly alongside. The fear and the hope, sometimes in the same hour.

It is not a medical book. It does not name any medication. The clinic prescribes the specifics. The planner records what they said.

## What's inside

Fifty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.** What this planner is, and what it is not.
- **Pre-cycle team page.** Reproductive endocrinologist, clinic, nurse, pharmacy, insurance contact. The numbers you will call again.
- **Pre-cycle calendar setup.** The dates the clinic gave you, mapped onto your life.
- **Pre-cycle cost setup.** What you have been told the cycle costs, what insurance covers, what you are paying out of pocket. A starting line.
- **Stim-phase daily tracker.** One page per day, tracking the injection by purpose, the side effects, the energy, the mood. No medication names.
- **Monitoring appointment log.** What they measured, what they said, what they want you to do next.
- **Retrieval day page.** What time. Who took you. What they told you after.
- **Fertilization and blastocyst report tracker.** Day 0, day 1, day 3, day 5, day 6. The numbers the clinic sends each morning.
- **Transfer day page** OR **freeze-all decision page.** Whichever your cycle calls for.
- **Two-week wait pages.** Four pages, slow.
- **Beta test day.** What time. What the number was. What it means.
- **Running cost tally.** Updated as you go.
- **"If it worked" page.**
- **"If it didn't" page.**
- **Partner page.** For the support person.
- **Telling people, or not telling people.** Scripts and decisions.
- **The "what I am angry about today" page.**
- **A final letter.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Print the whole thing, or print only what you need this cycle. Personal use only. You can re-print for the next cycle, if there is one.
