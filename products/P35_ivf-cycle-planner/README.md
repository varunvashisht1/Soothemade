# P35 — IVF Cycle Planner + Journal

A 50-page planner for one IVF cycle. Cycle tracker, medication log (by class/purpose only, no medicine names), monitoring-appointment log, retrieval and transfer pages, two-week wait, cost tracker, emotional journal, and the pages for whichever way the cycle ends.

## Why this exists

IVF is one of the most logistically intense things a person can do. Daily injections at specific times. Multiple monitoring appointments per week. Bloodwork. Egg counts. Embryo grades. Transfer dates. Beta tests. Each step has its own piece of paperwork from the clinic, and most planners do not handle the emotional weight or the cost tracking that runs alongside the clinical schedule.

This planner is for one cycle. It can be re-printed for the next cycle, if there is one.

## YMYL care

- IVF is a medical process. The planner does **not name a single medication**, brand or generic. Medications are tracked by purpose ("evening injection", "trigger shot", "luteal support") and route ("subcutaneous", "intramuscular", "oral"). The clinic prescribes the specifics. The planner only records what the clinic said.
- The planner does NOT prescribe a protocol. It does NOT recommend a clinic. It does NOT predict success or failure rates.
- Cover carries the YMYL banner.
- "Talk to your RE" appears on every protocol-decision page.
- A "when to call the clinic urgently" page exists for symptoms like severe pelvic pain, heavy bleeding, signs of hyperstimulation, or sudden weight gain.

## Scope

Approximately 50 pages, including:

- Cover + 3 front-matter pages
- Pre-cycle prep (team, calendar, costs, insurance, pharmacy)
- Stim-phase daily tracker (10 daily pages, more if your cycle runs longer)
- Monitoring appointment log (5 pages)
- Retrieval day page
- Fertilization / blastocyst report tracker
- Transfer day OR freeze-all page
- Two-week-wait pages (4 pages)
- Beta test day
- Cost running tally (whole-cycle)
- "If it worked" page
- "If it didn't" page
- Partner page
- Telling-or-not-telling page
- "What I am angry about today" page
- A final letter
