# P48 — Adoption Wait + Welcome Pack

A 30-page planner for adoptive parents — the long wait of the home-study and matching process, the financial planning, the grief that often comes with the adoption journey, the match call, the welcome day, the first week with the child. Honors the birth-family relationship throughout.

## Why this exists

Adoptive parents are largely invisible in postpartum content. Pregnancy planners assume biological pregnancy. Newborn planners assume birth from the parent reading. The adoption journey is structurally different (home study, matching, waiting, sometimes years; sometimes a call with hours of notice). This pack respects the difference.

## YMYL care

- Non-YMYL but tender.
- Birth-family relationships are described respectfully without prescribing how open / semi-open / closed your specific situation should be.
- The page on "how we will talk about our family" honors language conventions adoption advocates and adult adoptees recommend.
- No medication mentions.
- A small grief-page references infertility loss (common precursor to adoption) and routes to perinatal mental-health line.

## Scope

Approximately 30 pages:

- Cover, 3 front-matter pages
- "This is its own pregnancy" page
- Home study prep page
- Agency / lawyer / consultant tracker
- Financial planning (costs + tax credits)
- The grief page (infertility, prior loss, birth-family complexity)
- The wait — emotional check-ins page
- "What to do when a match falls through" page
- Match-call moment page
- Welcome-day plan
- First week with them
- Birth-family communication page
- "How we will talk about our family" page
- A final letter
