You are trying to get pregnant. You do not want everyone to know.

Maybe the in-laws ask too often. Maybe the roommate is nosy. Maybe the parents have opinions you do not want today. Maybe it is just for you, and not for the rest of the world. Maybe you have been trying for long enough that you do not want a pity-look every time someone walks by the kitchen counter.

This journal is for that. It looks like a generic daily-notes notebook. The cover does not say "fertility." The interior pages do not say "ovulation" or "cycle day 14." You, the buyer, know what each neutral field is for. You can do full tracking. Anyone glancing at it sees a wellness journal.

## What's inside

Sixty pages, fillable:

- **Cover.** "A Daily Notes Journal." No mention of fertility.
- **A note before you start.** What this journal is, and what it is not.
- **How to use this journal.** The neutral-language guide.
- **What each field is.** The decode. (At the back as well, can be torn out if you want.)
- **Six cycles of tracking.** Each cycle is six pages: a cycle overview, four weekly spreads, a reflection, a next-cycle-planning page. Numbered "Month 1" through "Month 6."
- **Pattern notes reference.**
- **Symptom dictionary.** Neutral.
- **When to talk to a provider.** If six months of tracking have not surfaced what you hoped.
- **A final note.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Print, fill, tear out the decode page if you want. Personal use only.
