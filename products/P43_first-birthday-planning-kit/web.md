The first birthday on the internet looks like a wedding. It is not a wedding. It is the kid turning one, with cake on their face and a few people who love them in the room.

This kit is for the version where the day feels like a milestone without you turning into a logistics manager. Six-week countdown. Theme ideas that take an afternoon. Guest planning. Food. Photos that are not the same Instagram shot everyone else takes.

## What's inside

Twenty pages, fillable:

- **A note before you start.** Permission to keep it small.
- **6-week countdown.** One page per week with the right amount of planning for that point.
- **Theme ideas.** Five low-effort, brand-aligned options. Pick one. Stop.
- **Guest list + invite tracker.**
- **Food + cake.** The standard "what we serve" list, with allergy notes.
- **Smash-cake-or-not.** A decision page.
- **Photo shot list.** With non-cliché options.
- **Memory keepers.** A letter to baby at one, a family quote-book template.
- **Day-of timeline.** So no one has to ask "when is X."
- **A final note.**

## Format

Instant-download PDF, US Letter + A4. Personal use only.
