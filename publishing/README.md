# Publishing Playbook

Built Day 10. Drives the slow-drip year of weekly publishing.

## Contents

- `calendar.md` — 52-week schedule mapping each week to: product to launch, blog post to publish, Pinterest pin to schedule, email to send. Optimized for seasonal timing.
- `weekly-checklist.md` — the 15–20 min Monday checklist. Open folder → publish Etsy → publish blog → schedule pin → log result.
- `runbook.md` — full operations manual. Covers: how to publish on Etsy, how to publish on Gumroad, how to deploy a new blog post, how to schedule Pinterest, how to respond to reviews, how to handle refunds, how to refresh SEO monthly.
- `metrics.md` — what to track weekly and the 90-day pivot triggers.
- `lead-magnets/` — the 6 lead magnets and their opt-in pages.
