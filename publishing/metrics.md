## Pivot trigger thresholds (defaults from runbook)

- **Q1 (Wk 13):** <3 sales total → diagnose with `toolkit/decision-trees/no-sales-by-q1.md`
- **Q2 (Wk 26):** <$200 revenue → niche pivot consideration
- **Q3 (Wk 39):** <$500 revenue → wind-down evaluation
- **Q4 (Wk 52):** <$1000 revenue → honest reset or close

## What NOT to track

To avoid sinking time into vanity metrics:

- ❌ Total page-views per day (noisy)
- ❌ Social media follower counts (we don't do organic social)
- ❌ Time spent per task (you'll over-optimize)
- ❌ Conversion-rate-by-day (too small a sample to be meaningful)
- ❌ Anything you can't action

Track only what you'd actually change behavior over.

## Where the numbers come from

- **Etsy:** Shop Manager → Stats (views, favorites, traffic sources, sales)
- **Gumroad:** Insights tab
- **Pinterest:** Pinterest Analytics → Performance
- **Blog:** Cloudflare Pages Web Analytics is free + privacy-respecting — enable in the Pages project settings. Plausible is a paid alternative if you outgrow it.
- **Email:** ConvertKit dashboard

## Tracking workflow (~5 min)

Every Monday at end of the weekly checklist:

1. Open Etsy stats
2. Note: views, favorites, sales for the just-published listing
3. Open Gumroad
4. Note: views, sales
5. Add one row to per-product log
6. Done

That's it. Don't over-engineer this.
