# 52-Week Publishing Calendar

> One product per week + one blog post per week + Pinterest pin batch. Designed for slow drip. Adjust the start date once Etsy account is verified.

## How to use this

- **Week N** below is your weekly drop. Each week takes ~20 min to publish if pre-built (see `publishing/weekly-checklist.md`).
- The order is optimized for: seasonal demand spikes, product-blog cross-pollination, and starting with the strongest Wave 1 products to capture the Etsy "new shop" boost window.
- Use the dates column for whatever year you're starting in.

## Seasonal logic

| Season | Surge | Calendar weight |
|---|---|---|
| Jan–Feb | "New year, new baby" + first trimester for Dec-conceived | Wave 1 launch, pregnancy planners |
| Mar–Apr | Baby shower season starts | Visitor scripts, partner playbook |
| May–Jul | "No phone summer," screen-free, sensory play peak | Screen-free + sensory cards prime |
| Aug–Sep | Back-to-school, routine charts, daycare prep | Maternity-leave-to-work, daycare drop-off |
| Oct–Nov | Pre-holiday postpartum support, Q4 cocooning | Postpartum recovery, "saying no" |
| Dec | Year-end memory keepers, gifts | Memory planners, gift bundles |

## Recommended start

Best Etsy launch month: **late February or early September** (Etsy shopping peaks in Q1 and back-to-school).

If you're starting *now* (any time), just open at week 1 and run. The seasonality below is general — adjust if your start month is, say, October vs February.

## What you do not launch every week

These weeks have product launches **paused** — you spend the time refreshing pins on prior listings, responding to messages, doing one quarterly review:

- Wk 2, 4, 6, 14, 17, 19, 22, 23 (Memorial Day), 29, 34, 42, 47 (Black Friday)

Holiday weeks (Wk 47 = Thanksgiving, Wk 51 = Christmas) — you can skip if life is overwhelming. The undated-product nature of this catalog means nothing is "missed."

## Pinterest pin batch logic

Per product, you get 5 pins from `marketing.md`. Schedule:
- 2 pins at launch
- 2 pins ~3 days later
- 1 final pin ~7 days later
- ElevenLabs Idea Pin: ~14 days after launch

Pinterest favors fresh pins. After 90 days, refresh the visuals on pins #1 and #5 of your top-3 performing listings. This is a 10-min Pro-plan task.

## Email cadence

If using ConvertKit:

- **At signup:** welcome email + lead magnet (postpartum reset audio)
- **+ 3 days:** first nurture email
- **+ 7 days:** second nurture email
- **+ 14 days, 21 days, 28 days, 35 days:** rest of the 7-email welcome sequence

Then biweekly: a soft-touch newsletter (3–4 sentence note + one product highlight + one "what's coming next week"). Total: ~26 emails/year.

## Tracking

Per week, log in `publishing/metrics.md`:
- Product launched
- Etsy views / favorites / sales (snapshot after 7 days)
- Gumroad views / sales
- Pinterest impressions / clicks
- Email subscribers added
- Notes on what worked / didn't

At weeks 13, 26, 39, 52 — write the quarterly review post.
